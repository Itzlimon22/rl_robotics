THESIS CLAIM (Discussion Section):
"A sensitivity analysis of the PID baseline revealed a fundamental limitation 
of linear control in extreme hydrodynamic environments. Low proportional gains 
(Kp < 15) resulted in sluggish responses leading to obstacle collisions, while 
high gains (Kp > 20) caused aggressive actuator saturation and rapid energy 
depletion without a meaningful increase in survivability. No configuration 
of the linear controller was able to approach the 100% success rate or the 
energy efficiency of the Curriculum Domain Randomization agent."
