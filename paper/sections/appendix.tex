\section{CDR Hyperparameter Sensitivity}
\label{app:sensitivity}

%% IF A9 sensitivity runs are complete — fill in real numbers:
We trained CDR with three window sizes ($W \in \{25, 50, 100\}$)
and the default thresholds, each with seed 0 for $500{,}000$ steps.
Table~\ref{tab:sensitivity} shows transfer success rate and energy
per step for each configuration.
All three window sizes achieve comparable success ($> 90\%$) and
similar energy ($[FILL]$--$[FILL]$), confirming that CDR performance
is robust to moderate variation in the window hyperparameter.
